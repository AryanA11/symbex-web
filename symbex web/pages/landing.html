Texlon

Texlon is a leading supplier of industrial textiles to the filtration, aerospace, film, composite, and lamination industries. Coated Fabrics Company is a trusted converter and supplier of plastic sheet and film, offering materials such as Rigid and Flexible Vinyl, APET, HIPS, HDPE, and Vinyl Coated and Banner Fabrics. CFC serves a wide range of industries—from packaging and dental to signage and industrial—by delivering high-quality, customizable solutions backed by responsive service and technical know-how.

CoastPad

CoastPad has grown from its roots as a molded shoulder pad maker into a leading global manufacturer of bra cups. CPT’s success is built on a foundation of innovation, consistency, and uncompromising quality. Today, we partner with top brands around the world to deliver performance-driven solutions in swimwear, activewear, and intimate apparel.

Thermech

Thermech is a specialty fluoropolymer coating and precision manufacturing company with a long-standing reputation for performance in mission-critical applications. Best known for its PTFE (Teflon) coatings, Thermech also offers a wide range of proprietary blends used in medical, aerospace, defense, and advanced commercial industries. In addition to coatings, Thermech excels in compression molding and machining of custom fluoropolymer parts for harsh environments. Thermech supports its customer’s innovation through quality, flexibility, and solutions for evolving market demands.

Stainless Micro-Polish

Stainless Micro-Polish is a trusted leader in precision stainless steel finishing.  Founded on a proprietary process that polishes, passivates, deburrs, and ultra-cleans while preserving micro tolerances, the company has set the industry standard for quality and consistency in the medical, chromatography, semi-conductor, and aerospace industries.

CrystalForm

CrystalForm is a leading provider of high-performance plastic films and sheets for Visual Packaging applications, including Thermoforms, Folded Boxes, and Print Substrates. Our product line features APET, Rigid PVC, and PETG materials, including sustainable options with high levels of post-consumer recycled content to meet both performance and environmental goals. With over 50 years of technical expertise and a strong distribution network across North America, we offer vendor-managed solutions that streamline supply chains and support lean manufacturing.

Interlayer Technologies

Interlayer Technologies is a California-based company specializing in vapor deposition and advanced surface modification for medical devices and laboratory consumables. By using methods such as plasma-enhanced chemical vapor deposition (PECVD), they help customers transform low-cost components into high-performance products with superior function and reliability. Interlayer partners with medtech and IVD teams to optimize surfaces for diagnostic accuracy, device strength, and miniaturization—often unlocking entirely new product categories. Our mission is to deliver scalable, cost-effective solutions that elevate both product performance and patient outcomes.
